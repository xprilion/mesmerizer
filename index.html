MESMERIZER'17

Rebirth

Mesmerizer, over the years has seen prolific and stupendous response from colleges all over Bengal. One of the incredible college festival as it stands out to be. This festival has seen budding as well as famous talents across the city of joy and our state cherishing its existence. The event has staged names that bear a strong name in the talent industry of our tri-colour nation. With the academic excellence that our college is named for the cultural excellence is no-less to be talked about.
Well here we try to make and bring a difference with everything that we do, it’s a win to win, rejoice to rejoice for all who are a part of the festival in all manners. Sponsorship is building a relation, which is just a word for us. But we stride to build a family and we have procured that since time immemorial. We stride to rejoice and strive for the excellence of cultural activities across the city and beyond it as well. The bond that we ask you to join in is for an important cause: the arts.
Each event that we do has the key of success for the participants and the audience segment. It provides mirth, entertainment and engages us to gather ‘wow’ from all. Supporting us would be like supporting our local as well as national arts, heritage and cultural aspect. This will definitely provide you an unforgettable experience.
